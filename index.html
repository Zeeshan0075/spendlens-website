What's inside

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for people who want to know where their money goes — without becoming a product themselves.

⚡️

Log in 5 seconds

Two taps from home screen to logged expense. Quick-add widget, Action Button, or Siri — your call.

🌍

55 currencies, native

Track AED, INR, USD, GBP — all together. Live exchange rates from frankfurter.app, cached for offline.

📷

Receipt scanner

Snap a receipt — Apple's Vision framework reads the total, merchant, and date. Entirely on your device.

📊

Budgets that breathe

Set a budget, get gentle nudges as you approach it. Roll over what you didn't spend. No guilt-trips.

📈

Trends that talk

See category-by-category month-over-month changes with sparklines. Spot the spending creep early.

🪟

5 widget sizes

Small, medium, large, and lock screen. Today's spend, monthly total, or budget progress — at a glance.

🗣

Siri & Shortcuts

"Hey Siri, log 45 dirhams for coffee." Done. Built on App Intents — works with the Action Button too.

📤

Export anytime

CSV for spreadsheets. PDF reports for accountants. Your data is always yours to take with you.

☁️

iCloud sync

Optional sync across iPhone and iPad through your private iCloud container. Apple can't see it. We can't either.
